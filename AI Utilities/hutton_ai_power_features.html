Useful features colleagues may miss

Power features hidden in plain sight

Some of the most useful things in the hub are not separate tools. They are options inside utilities.

Translation & Style Converter

Change language

Translate text for pupils or families. Use simplification if the original text is too dense.

Stay in English

Convert text into a different tone or format without translating it.

Change format

Turn text into a formal report, parent email, letter, webpage, social media-style message, newspaper article, text message or pupil-friendly explanation.

Example: paste a dense paragraph about homework and convert it into a parent-friendly message, then ask for a shorter text-message version.

Adaptive teaching features

Instruction Simplifier

Turns complex task instructions into clearer, shorter steps.

Scaffold Generator

Creates sentence starters, structure strips, hints or guided support.

Reading Age Tool

Helps adapt text while keeping the subject meaning intact.

Text-to-Speech Tool

Creates a version of text that works better when read aloud.

Dual Coding Generator

Pairs simplified text with visual cues and labelled ideas.

WAGOLL Generator

Creates model answers that pupils can analyse, improve or imitate.

Small prompts that make a big difference

  • “Make this suitable for Year 8 boys at a UK secondary school.”
  • “Keep the subject knowledge accurate but reduce the reading demand.”
  • “Include common misconceptions.”
  • “Make the task possible without teacher help.”
  • “Give me a version suitable for parents.”
